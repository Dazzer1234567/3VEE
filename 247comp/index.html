What 24:7 Comp does
01
See what you're processing

Live spectrum, signal trail and gain reduction overlaid on the dynamics curve. Stop guessing — you can finally see the move you're making.

02
Comp + Exp in one curve

Up and down dynamics on a single editable curve. Shape transients, lift quiet detail and tame loud parts without stacking three plug-ins.

03
Node-based curve editor

Drop nodes, drag knees, lock points. Per-band EQ-style dynamics control with the precision of a curve and the speed of a knob.

04
Smart sidechain

Built-in sidechain EQ and length window. Trigger off exactly what you want — not what happens to be loudest.
